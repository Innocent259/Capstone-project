73rd FIFA Congress Meeting

The 73rd FIFA Congress will be held in Kigali, Rwanda from 13th to 17th March 2023. The 2023 FIFA presidential election will take place at the 73rd FIFA Congress, the call for which was approved by the FIFA Council during its meeting on 30 March 2022. Thousands of delegates are expected to participate at the event in Remarkable Rwanda. Kigali already hosted a meeting of the FIFA Council in October 2018.
